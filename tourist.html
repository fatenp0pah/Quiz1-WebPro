Explore Kedah

Langkawi Island
Discover the tropical paradise of Langkawi Island, where pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush rainforests await. Whether you're relaxing on the sun-drenched shores of Pantai Cenang, soaring above the island on the Langkawi Sky Bridge, or exploring the natural wonders of the Kilim Karst Geoforest Park, Langkawi offers a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and scenic beauty. Plus, enjoy duty-free shopping and luxurious resorts for an unforgettable getaway in Malaysia’s Jewel of Kedah.
Kilim Geoforest Park
Kilim Karst Geoforest Park, a UNESCO Global Geopark in Langkawi, is a stunning natural wonder featuring ancient limestone formations, dense mangrove forests, and diverse wildlife. Visitors can explore its unique landscape by boat, cruising through serene rivers surrounded by towering cliffs and intricate cave systems. The park is home to fascinating wildlife, including eagles, macaques, and vibrant marine life. Highlights include Bat Cave, Crocodile Cave, and the iconic Eagle Feeding experience. It's a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure seekers looking to experience Langkawi's untouched beauty and ecological diversity.
Mahsuri's Tomb
Mahsuri's Tomb, or Makam Mahsuri, is a historic site in Langkawi that commemorates the legendary Mahsuri, who was wrongfully executed for adultery over 200 years ago. According to folklore, she cursed the island with seven generations of bad luck before her death. The site features her tomb and a museum showcasing artifacts from Langkawi’s past, offering a glimpse into its cultural heritage and Mahsuri’s tragic tale.
Alor Setar Tower
Menara Alor Setar is an iconic telecommunications tower in Alor Setar, Kedah, standing 165.5 meters tall. It serves as both a functional tower and a popular tourist attraction, offering panoramic views of the city, surrounding rice fields, and even the distant Langkawi Island from its observation deck. The tower also features a revolving restaurant, providing diners with a unique experience. As one of the tallest towers in Malaysia, Menara Alor Setar is a symbol of the city's modernization and a must-visit for those exploring Kedah.
