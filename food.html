Local Food in Kedah

Laksa Utara

The broth of Laksa Kedah is typically made with a base of fish (often mackerel) cooked in a rich coconut milk and a blend of spices, including turmeric, lemongrass, and chili.

Kuih Karas

Crispy, sweet kuih that resembles fried vermicelli. It's often shaped like a flower or other decorative designs.

Gulai Temenung

Features temenung fish (mackerel) cooked in a spicy coconut milk-based curry, served with steamed rice.

Pek Nga

**Pek Nga** is a traditional Kedah dish made from a simple batter of grated coconut, flour, and water, pan-fried to create a crispy, coconut-flavored pancake. It's typically enjoyed with curry, sambal, or sugar for a savory or sweet taste. Often served as a snack or breakfast, Pek Nga is loved for its light, crispy texture and rich coconut flavor.

Nasi Ulam Kedah

Nasi ulam is a popular traditional dish in Kedah. Just like the name suggests, the rice is mixed with the shredded ulam and paired it with keladi masak asam as side dish. Unlike nasi kerabu from Kelantan, nasi ulam is white in colour.
