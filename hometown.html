Kedah, Malaysia

About Kedah

Kedah is known as the "Rice Bowl of Malaysia" due to its extensive rice cultivation. The state is rich in history and culture, offering a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions.

Attractions

  • Langkawi Island
  • Kilim Geoforest Park
  • Mahsuri's Tomb
  • Alor Setar Tower

Culture

Kedah's culture is influenced by Malay traditions and customs, with vibrant festivals, traditional music, and delicious local cuisine such as Nasi Lemak and Laksa Kedah.
